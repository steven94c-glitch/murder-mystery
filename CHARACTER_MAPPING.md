## Who's Who - The New Cast

### **The Host & Killer**
- **Arabella Ashford-Cross** (formerly Grammy) — The legendary art collector. Hosts the gala. Has the most to lose. THE KILLER.

### **The Victim**
- **Vladimir Karpov** (formerly Derek) — The sophisticated art dealer. Arrives with dangerous secrets. Gets poisoned.

### **Key Witnesses**
- **Vivienne Dumont** (formerly Liz) — The glamorous jazz singer. Sees Arabella's crucial moment. Best witness to the crime.

### **Observers**
- **Maximilian Creed** (formerly Steve) — The witty playwright. Analytical. Notices inconsistencies.
- **Quinton Darling** (formerly Annie) — The wealthy aesthete. Observant about human nature. Amused by the drama.
- **Margot Silvain** (formerly Nan) — The sophisticated black market dealer. Understands leverage. Sees what's coming.

### **Suspects with Strong Motive**
- **Evangeline Beaumont** (formerly Doug) — The young heiress. Vladimir threatened her inheritance. Scared.
- **Constantine Blackwell** (formerly John) — The private investigator. Vladimir threatened his cases. Methodical.
- **Josephine Marchmont** (formerly Kelly) — The gossip columnist. Vladimir threatened her credibility. Career at risk.
- **Celestine Vanderbilt** (formerly Evan) — The rival collector. Vladimir threatened her reputation. Competitive.

### **Red Herring**
- **Ludovic Steele** (formerly Clark) — The charming con artist. Vladimir had leverage on him. Wanted him dead but didn't act.

## Why the Mapping Matters

Each new character inherited the **role mechanics** from the original but with completely new personalities and motivations:

- **Arabella** has Arabella's burden of protecting her legacy (like Grammy protecting her family), but through art reputation instead of family wealth
- **Vladimir** has Derek's role as the blackmailer holding leverage, but through art authentication instead of financial crimes
- **Vivienne** has Liz's position as the key witness who sees the crucial moment
- **Ludovic** has Clark's red herring status — has motive but doesn't act
- All the other suspects have different secrets, but the same structure: Vladimir discovered something, they panicked, Arabella solved it

You can reference this while hosting to remember who corresponds to what role from the original game.
